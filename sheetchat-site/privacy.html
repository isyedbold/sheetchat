1. Information We Collect

SheetChat collects the following information:

  • Usage Data: Commands and interactions with the application for improving service quality

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All interactions with Google Sheets happen through official Google APIs
  • Authentication tokens are handled securely and never logged
  • We use industry-standard encryption and security practices

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights or the safety of our users
  • With your explicit consent

5. Google API Services

SheetChat's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke access to your Google account at any time through Google's account settings
  • Request deletion of any data we may have stored
  • Contact us with questions about your data

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the effective date.
